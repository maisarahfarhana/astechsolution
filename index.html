Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our web development and design services.

Web development companies design, build, and maintain websites and web applications. We handle everything from frontend user interfaces and user experience (UI/UX) to robust backend architecture, database integration, security setups, and deployment so your business has a high-performing digital footprint.
a. Stage 1

The first step in web development process is to understand your unique ideas and requirement. We will perform research and go through a proposal showcase to you. Once the information is being gathered and confirmed, we will proceed to the next web development process.

b. Stage 2

The next step is mock-up presentation. You are able to visualize the overall look and feel of the website. This step helps to fasten the web development process.

c. Stage 3

After the mock-up is being approved, we will kick-start the web development. Our team will provide a weekly progress update to keep you informed.

d. Stage 4

Once the web development is completed, Our internal QA team will perform testing on the overall website and make necessary changes. We will pass the development to you for UAT (user acceptance testing) together with training session.

Fully custom web development means building a website or web application entirely from scratch based on your unique business needs, specifications, and branding—without relying on rigid pre-made templates or restrictive plugins. This ensures maximum performance, scalability, and unique user experiences.
Partnering with a local development company provides the advantage of clear communication, deep understanding of the local market landscape and consumer behavior, easier alignment on time zones, and tailored digital strategies designed to help your business stand out regionally and globally.
We offer continuous post-launch support and maintenance to ensure your platform remains secure, up-to-date, and fully optimized. Our dedicated team is ready to assist you with system updates, technical troubleshooting, and performance enhancements whenever needed.
The timeline for a custom website development project varies depending on the scope, complexity, and specific feature requirements. Typically, standard projects range from a few weeks to a couple of months, during which we provide structured milestones and regular progress updates.
Responsive web design ensures that your website adapts seamlessly to all screen sizes—whether smartphones, tablets, or desktop computers. This is critical for capturing mobile traffic, improving search engine optimization (SEO), and delivering an optimal user experience. We build fully responsive interfaces from the ground up to ensure your brand looks exceptional across every device.
